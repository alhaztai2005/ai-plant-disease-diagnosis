    Disease Library

    Detectable Conditions

    Trained on thousands of annotated images covering the most destructive plant diseases worldwide.

    🍂

    Early Blight

    Dark concentric ring lesions on older leaves causing yellowing and early drop. Spreads rapidly in warm, wet weather.

    Medium Risk
    🌫️

    Late Blight

    Water-soaked spots turning dark brown with white mold. Can destroy entire crops within days if untreated.

    High Risk
    🟤

    Leaf Mold

    Pale green-yellow spots above with olive-brown velvet mold below. Common in high-humidity greenhouses.

    Medium Risk
    🔵

    Septoria Leaf Spot

    Small circular spots with dark borders and light centers, causing significant early defoliation.

    Medium Risk
    🦠

    Bacterial Spot

    Angular water-soaked spots spreading via rain splash. Affects both foliage and fruit quality severely.

    High Risk
    🕷️

    Spider Mites

    Tiny pests causing stippled bronze leaves with fine webbing. Thrives in hot, dry conditions and spreads fast.

    Medium Risk
    🌀

    Mosaic Virus

    Mottled light-dark green mosaic on leaves. Spread by aphids — no cure, only prevention and removal.

    High Risk
    🌿

    Healthy Plant

    No disease detected! Your plant shows vibrant healthy foliage with normal growth patterns. Keep it up!
